Our roots are firmly planted in the realm of editorial design, and our creative endeavors are driven by an unwavering commitment to contemporary art, culture, design, and cutting-edge technologies. Over the years, our dedication to excellence has garnered numerous international design awards, provided opportunities to showcase our work in prestigious exhibitions, and granted us the honor of presenting our own initiative at the Venice Biennale.
With our close-knit, international team, we engage with a diverse clientele, ranging from cultural institutions and luxury fashion houses to commercial enterprises. Our design services span a wide spectrum, and we take great pride in our enduring partnerships, offering comprehensive graphic design solutions to esteemed organizations such as Bayerische Staatsoper, Haus der Kunst, and Villa Stuck.
At our core, we are deeply passionate about editorial projects, which encompass over a decade of continuous creative direction for esteemed publications like Zeit Magazin and Numéro Berlin. Our portfolio also boasts the development of global campaigns for industry giants such as Nike and Apple. We have had the privilege of collaborating with Supreme NY on seasonal apparel graphics and creative services.
Bureau Borsche’s expertise extends to branding, and we have had the opportunity of partnering with renowned brands, including Adidas, Balenciaga, Birkenstock, BMW, Givenchy, Louis Vuitton, On Running, NGG, Moncler, Rimowa, Telfar, Farfetch and Highsnobiety.
In 2021, we realized a long-standing dream by collaborating with one of Europe’s premier football clubs, Internazionale Milano, to craft their new visual identity. Our passionate work for football clubs like Venezia FC, Kallithea FC, and Internazionale Milano has encompassed full redesigns and the creation of striking new jersey designs, further exemplifying our dedication to the intersection of design and sports culture.
