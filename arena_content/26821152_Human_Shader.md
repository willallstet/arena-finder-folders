1CLAIM PIXELS
Claim a random pixel for yourself, and you'll get its X and Y coordinates together with a unique Code that marks the pixels as yours, and yours only, for the next 8 hours. If you don't submit the pixel back within that time period, it will get freed again for others to claim. You can claim up to 4 pixels at a time.
Claim a Pixel
2COMPUTE
With the X and Y coordinates of your pixel you'll compute its R, G and B color values by following the Worksheet below. You must perform these operations by hand, no computers or calculators allowed!
In fact, you'll need to take a Photo of the sheet of paper where you performed the computations, where we see all additions and intermediary long multiplications, not just the values of the variables. Here is an example of a valid photo.
Get Worksheet
