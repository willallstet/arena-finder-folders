Urban Armor is a collection of DIY wearable electronics that investigate the relationship between technology, the body, and personal and public space. Find instructions and code to make your own pieces below. Feel free to take elements, change them, and make them your own. If you make something you’re excited about, I’d love to hear about it at info@urbanarmor.org. Please be responsible and safe, always use proper safety equipment and always ask for help if you need it. Happy making!
