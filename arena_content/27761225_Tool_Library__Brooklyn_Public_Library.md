Borrow a tool from Greenpoint Library!The Greenpoint Library Tool Library was created with the knowledge that repair and maintenance have a meaningful role to play in environmental and planetary health. Born out of Repair Hub, a Brooklyn Public Library Incubator project, Greenpoint Library's Tool Library empowers patrons to borrow tools to work on home projects, repair their possessions and learn new tool skills. In making tools accessible to the community, Brooklyn Public Library is promoting environmental responsibility, sustainability and equity for all.Our ToolsPower Tools Hand Tools All ToolsTool Library Lending PolicyBrooklyn Public Library patrons with an adult library card may borrow up to three tools at a time. This number includes one power tool.  This free service is currently available at the Greenpoint branch by following these steps:Place your tool on hold by using the links in the tool list below or by navigating to our catalog and searching there. Greenpoint is the only branch you may pick up or return your tool.When your tool is ready for pickup, we will notify you by email and you may come and pick it up. You must show ID with current address. Tools not picked up within 7 days will be taken off hold.When you are done with your tool, or when your week is up, please return it to the reference desk at Greenpoint Library. Please make sure to return cleaned tools with all their components, in the original case/box. Tools may not be left in book drops.Frequently Asked QuestionsHow do I request to borrow a tool?You may place a hold on the tool you would like to borrow.May I borrow a tool the day I put it on hold?No, you must wait until you receive a notification that the tool is ready for pickup.How many tools may I borrow?You may borrow three tools, including one power tool.Where may I borrow a tool?Tools are only available for pickup and return at Greenpoint Library.How long may I borrow the tool?All tools may be borrowed for one week.Can I renew the tool?Yes, as long as there are no other patron holds on the tool.How long will you hold the tool for me?Tools will be held for seven days.Do I have to fill out a liability waiver to borrow the tool?Yes, the first time you borrow a tool you must sign a liability waiver.How and where do I check out a tool?After you've received an email confirmation that the tool is ready for pickup, you may ask for your tool at Greenpoint Library's front reference desk. You will be asked to sign a waiver, show your ID and proof of current address. There are no same-day-request pickups.Can someone else pick up a tool for me?No, only the cardholder may pick up the tool.Can I pick up or return a tool at any time?You must wait to pick up a tool until you have received notice that the tool is available. You may return the tool during Greenpoint Library's open hours and please do not return in the book drop.May I borrow a tool if I have missing or outstanding materials on my card?If you have more than $15 in billed tool materials, you may not borrow a tool until your account is in good standing.After I am notified a tool is ready for me, how long do I have to pick it up?You have seven days to pick up the tool.What happens if I return a tool late?You will receive an email reminding you of your late tool. After 14 days past the return date, tools will be marked as missing, and a replacement fee will be assessed and added to your account.Replacement fees depend on the cost of the tool to be replaced and will block the patron from borrowing, renewing, and making holds for other library materials in the interim. This fee will be removed from the account upon return of the tool.Please return tools and don't let them gather dust in your home! This is a community Tool Library, and we want to ensure access of the tools to our neighbors.Can I donate tools to the library?At this time, we are not taking donations.This project is made possible by the generous support of the Greenpoint Community Enviornmental Fund.
