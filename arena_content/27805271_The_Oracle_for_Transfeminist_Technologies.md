this oracle is a hands-on card deck designed to help us collectively envision and share ideas for transfeminist technologies from the future.
throughout history, human beings have used a wide variety of divination procedures – such as tarot decks – as technologies to understand the present and reshape our destinies.
the wisdom of the oracle, embedded with transfeminist values, will help us foresee a future where technologies are designed by people who are too often excluded from or targeted by technology in today's world.playi have provided the cards below.
