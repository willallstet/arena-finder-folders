Ocean Data Explorer
To meet our mission of providing accurate and reliable data for decision making, AOOS has developed the Ocean Data Explorer (ODE) – over 3,000 marine data layers to visualize and access.
This portal contains scientific and management information on the biological, chemical and physical characteristics of Alaska and its surrounding waters.

Real-time Sensor Map
The AOOS real-time sensor catalog compiles current meteorologic and oceanographic data for the State of Alaska from real-time in situ sensors. These include weather stations, stream gauges, moorings, etc.

AOOS Data Certification and Management
AOOS maintains current Regional Coastal Ocean System (RCOS) certification necessary to coordinate non-federal observing assets and disseminate associated data in the Alaska Region.
More information about AOOS and IOOS data certification
The goal of the AOOS Data Assembly Center (DAC) and Data Management subsystem is to acquire, archive and share marine data and information products to meet the needs of Alaska stakeholders and the national IOOS program. AOOS contracts Axiom Data Science to provide data management with the goal of increasing the accessibility, reuse, and impact of scientific data.

All products published on this website are prototype products and are not intended to be used for navigational or operational purposes. This website provides an aggregation and display service for distributed data and modeling products that come from a wide array of contributors. We are grateful to those contributors and make every attempt to give them credit for their products. We welcome suggestions for clarification or correction, as appropriate.
