About Me
My name is Lucía (she/her) and I am a Brussels based artist, musician and computer scientist.
My current interests are handmade electronics instruments, hosting intimate music rituals, and designing webpages. You can find more about my work in my social media or by discovering the links this website's garden.
I make music as Xaxalxe (soundcloud, bandcamp) and I am a resident on Lyl Radio where I have a bimonthly show called Half Step Outside. You can also listen all of my older mixes.
